ABOUT DEERWOOD FARM

Deerwood Farm is a private, family owned and operated facility in the heart of Barrington Hills, IL. Located 30 miles northwest of Chicago.

Since building our new facility in 2007, we have successfully trained and shown hunters, jumpers, equitation and ponies to many IHJA championships both at rated (A) and unrated shows (B).

What We Do

At Deerwood Farm we specialize in training the horse and rider in a safe but fun environment where everyone is like family. Our program emphasises basic horsemanship skills for even the youngest riders. Everyone is taught proper handling techniques both on the ground and in the saddle so we can saftely enjoy the sport while maximizing learning.


We are proud of our abilities at matching children and adults with exceptionally talented ponies and horses so they can acheive their goals in the show ring.


Deerwood Farm always has a nice selection of young prospects and seasoned show horses for sale and lease at all times.
